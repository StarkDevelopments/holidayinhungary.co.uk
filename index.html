This superbly furnished and decorated newly built property for 8+1 people is located in a quiet residential area, less than 20 minutes stroll away from the beach. Climate

There are four seasons, with a very warm summer (max. 38C) from Jun-Aug. Spring and Autumn are mild, whilst winters are very cold.

About Vonyarcvashegy

Vonyarcvashegy is located on the northern side of Lake Balaton, at the foot of the Keszthely Hills. It is one of the most popular bathing resorts amongst foreign and Hungarian tourists alike, due to its location and geographical features. The sandy part of its beach is perfect for children to play in and the watersports available here include windsurfing, canoeing, and wakeboarding or waterskiing on the 820m long pentagon shaped course. You can find a wide range of restaurants, cafes, small supermarkets, gift and clothes shops, tennis courts, a nightclub, tourist information office and a post office nearby. You can go fishing, cycling on the cycle path or go walking in the nearby hills using the footpaths that lead into a national park. You can also visit the church on top of Szent Mihaly Hill, which has spectacular views over the lake.

About Lake Balaton

The "Hungarian Sea", is the peoples name for the 50 mile long lake with silky green-yellow water in the middle of Transdanubia. Lake Balaton is one of Hungarys most precious treasures and most frequented resort. It is the largest lake in Central Europe. At its center, the depth is 52.5 ft. The summer water temperature of around 80F is warmer than the air in the morning and in the evening. The water and the silky mud of the lake are very good remedies for nervous complaints, anemia and nervous fatigue.
