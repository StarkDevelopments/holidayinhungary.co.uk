Map & overview of other places of interest.

KESZTHELY (3km)

Is the largest and oldest settlement of Lake Balaton. The beautiful Festetics Palace surrounded by an English park, is the third largest chateau in the country. Its library contains 86 thousand books. Weekly concerts are organized in the music hall of mirrors.

HEVIZ (9km)

Is the second largest hot-water lake in the world, at a temperature of 90F, open-air bathing is very pleasant even in winter. Massage and treatments are also available.

SZIGLIGET (20km)

Has a unique atmosphere with its houses creeping up the hillside and its green gardens. There is a stunning panorama from the ruins of the 13th-century Szigliget Fortress on Castle Hill. The Esterhazy Palace is now a house for writers. There are more than150 types of pine trees in its well-kept park.

TIHANY (60km)

The twin-towered church of the Tihany peninsula is distinctly visible far and wide. A very charming village with spectacular views over the lake.




There are lots of other interesting places in the area, relevant brochures can be found at the house or the local tourist office.
