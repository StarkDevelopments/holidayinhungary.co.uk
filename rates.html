Rates

Costs range between £400 - £600.

Check price & availability.

Towels

Available on request at £3 per person per week. Payable in cash on arrival.

Booking & Payment

We are a registered member of villarenters.com, and all our bookings go through their centralized booking system.

A 50% non-refundable deposit is payable at the time of booking via the booking system. The remaining 50% is payable 28 days prior to arrival.

Cancellation

Deposit not refundable should you cancel your booking. Should you cancel your booking within the periods stated below, the deposit and % of balance stated are forfeit.

3 weeks prior to arrival: Deposit + 60%

2 weeks prior to arrival: Deposit + 80%

1 week prior to arrival: Deposit + 100%

Breakage Deposit

At the time the final balance is paid a breakage deposit of £150 will be authorised to your payment card, which acts as security for the holiday home being left in a tidy and presentable state and for any loss and/or damage to the property and its contents. The authorisation to deduct any payment up to £150 from the card will remain in effect for 14 days after your holiday has concluded.

Arrival Times

The rental period normally runs from 16.00 (4 pm) Saturday to 10.00 (10 am) Saturday.

Responsibilities of The Tenant

The tenant has a responsibility to treat the holiday home with care and respect, and is responsible for any damage caused by any member of their party. The tenant has a duty to leave the holiday home clean and tidy; to wash up, empty fridge, freezer and rubbish bins before departure.

The tenant is responsible for personal and physical damage during the rental period.

No single sex goups.
